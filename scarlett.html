A Study In Scarlett

Author:Sir Arthur Conan Doyle

A Study In Scarlet is the first book feautring Sherlock Holmes.It was published in 1887.

Book Blurb

The story marks the first appearance of Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson, who would become the most famous detective duo in literature.
             The book's title derives from a speech given by Holmes, a consulting detective, to his friend and chronicler Watson on the nature of
              his work, in which he describes the story's murder investigation as his "study in scarlet": "There's the scarlet thread of murder
               running through the colourless skein of life, and our duty is to unravel it, and isolate it, and expose every inch of it."Wikipedia
